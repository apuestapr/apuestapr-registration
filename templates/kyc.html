Instructions

  1. Click on "Begin KYC" when the customer has presented you with identification.
  2. Follow the instructions below until you get a message saying that the KYC process was successful.
  3. If the KYC process fails, you can override it by clicking on "Continue to Registration". Note that in this case, you must review alternate identification and provide details in the box on the next screen.
  4. If KYC is successful, you can click on "Continue to Registration" to fill in the customer's details.
